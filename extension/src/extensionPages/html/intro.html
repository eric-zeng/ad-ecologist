AdEcologist Research Study Instructions

Please read this whole page before beginning

Thank you for participating in our research study and installing our extension! The study instructions are given below, but we will guide you through as you go, so you don't need to remember all the steps. Here's how the study will work:

On the next page, we will provide you a list of websites to visit.
One-by-one, please click and open each website. When you open a website from the list, you will see this popup:


Click the "Start" button to start the data collection process. Our extension will automatically collect data on the ads on the page. You may see the popup briefly disappear and reappear.

After the extension scans all of the ads on the page, it will automatically refresh and scan the page a second time.

While the extension is scanning a page:

  • Please do not open or switch to new tabs or windows in the web browser.
  • It is ok to use your phone or other apps on your computer extension is collecting data, such as Zoom or Microsoft Word.
  • If there is another popup on the web page that is not the extension popup, please close it.

After you have visited each website, we'll ask you to fill out a short survey about the ads we collected.

At the end, you'll have a chance to choose which (if any) ad screenshots you would not like to share with us. This is in case an ad is extremely specific to you, and you feel uncomfortable sharing the screenshot of the ad with us.

Once you have finished collecting data on all sites, we will send you a link to Prolific with the completion code.
