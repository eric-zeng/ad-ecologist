What kinds of data will we collect?

  1. Data about the ads you see (only on the sites we ask you to visit)
    • The URL of the website the ad was shown on
    • Screenshot of the ad
    • Amount paid by the advertisers to show you the ad
    • Data about web trackers and analytics on the site
  2. We will also use demographic info already collected from you in the prescreening survey.

You will have the option to decide if you want to let us collect the ad information on a given web page. If you feel that the ads are too personal or don't want to share the ad screenshots for some other reason, you can specify which ads you want to exclude after finishing all sites and we won't collect them.


This study was determined to be exempt for Human Subjects Regulations by the University of Washington Institutional Review Board (IRB), meaning that it involves no more than minimal risk to you.
If you have any questions about this study, you can contact the researchers at uwadstudy@cs.washington.edu
